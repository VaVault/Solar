Welcome 👋

Explore a simplified, to-scale-ish 3D model of our Solar System. Drag to orbit, scroll/pinch to zoom, and right-drag to pan. Click any planet to learn more.

How this model works

Orbits are drawn in the ecliptic plane and planets move on circular paths with realistic relative orbital periods. Distances and sizes are scaled so everything fits on screen.
